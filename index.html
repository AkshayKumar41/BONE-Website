About Us

BONE International LLC is a technology-based sportswear company founded in Ann Arbor, Michigan, and we’re dedicated to creating innovative exercise accessories for the everyday man.

Everyone deserves to enjoy a healthier lifestyle without discomfort or distraction. BONE combines smart technology and sportswear design to produce high-quality, practical athletic wear. We are committed to upholding innovation and high standards to make fitness more accessible, enjoyable, and fulfilling.

Mission

We want to empower our customers by providing enhanced exercise experience and pursuit of health and wellness.

Vision

We envision a world where everyone can exercise with the fear of pain or discomfort, moving forward toward a healthier society.

Goal

BONE aims to become a fierce competitor in the sportswear industry, bringing technological innovation to their customers.
